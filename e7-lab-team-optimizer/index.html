Show Instructions

  • Select a hero on the left and click on "Add Hero to Roster" to create a roster of any size more than four heroes. Click on "Calculate" to see the teams with the highest morale.
  • You may lock a hero using the icon. Locking a hero will make sure your teams will always contain the locked heroes. You can lock up to three heroes.
  • You may also use advanced features such as increasing team size and adding contraints to your team. You can add class contraints for Soul Weavers, Knights and Other classes.
  • If there are too many heroes and your browser crashes from calculations, check the "Use Advanced Features" box to enable back-end server use.
